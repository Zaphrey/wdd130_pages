Difficulty: Beginner

The Anna Ruby Falls trail is located near Helen, GA. The hike is approximately 0.9 miles round trip with an elevation gain of ~219ft. It features twin waterfalls going over a tall cliff near the summit of Tray Mountain.

Difficulty: Intermediate

The Pig Trail is located near the Macon Children's Home in Bibb, Georgia. It is approximately 7.4 Miles and is a loop styled trail. Estimated to be completed within 2hr30m and features an elevation gain of ~374ft. It's a popular trail for hiking, mountain biking, and running.

Difficulty: Hard

Mt Yonah Trail is an out-and-back styled trail located near Cleveland, Georgia. It is considered one of the hardest trails in Georgia by other hikers, and features a round trip length of 4.2 miles with an elevation gain of ~1,453ft.
